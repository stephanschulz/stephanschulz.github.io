## Image Guidelines:

- **Format**: JPG or PNG (JPG preferred for photos)
- **Resolution**: At least 600x400px (higher is better)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 3:2 recommended (e.g., 1200x800, 1800x1200)
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB for faster loading (use compression tools if needed)

## Compression Tools:

- **Online**: TinyPNG (https://tinypng.com/)
- **Mac**: ImageOptim (https://imageoptim.com/)
- **Command line**: `convert input.jpg -quality 85 -resize 1200x output.jpg`

Once you add the images here, they'll automatically display on your website!
