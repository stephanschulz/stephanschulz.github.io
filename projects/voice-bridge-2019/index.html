"Voice Bridge" Interactive installation by Rafael Lozano-Hemmer. Two intercoms control 440 light fixtures live, with a time dimension. When the piece is not being used each light glimmers to the sound of a different voice recording from the past. Blue Bridge in Gran Rapids, Michigan. Part of Project1 Exhibition by ArtPrize.
