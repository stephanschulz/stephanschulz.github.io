Kristallstimmen is a permanent interactive installation designed for Swarovskiâ€™s â€œCrystal Worlds Museumâ€ in Wattens, Austria. The piece features an array of 3,000 suspended loudspeakers, each clad in hundreds of small black crystals that get illuminated when in use. As visitors traverse the space, sensors detect their presence and turn on the speaker directly above them, which lights up and plays a voice message. Each loudspeaker contains a recording from a different employee from around the world, speaking in their native language about their relationship to crystal, with over 100 languages represented. When no visitor is present, the installation is silent.
