An interactive sound and light installation designed to transform the 2,000 year old "Augusta Raurica" Roman Theatre in Augst, near Basel in Switzerland. Live and curated voice messages activated the piece using hundreds of glimmering lights and loudspeakers arranged in concentric rings that underlined the architecture of sound travel.
